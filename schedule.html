2016 Session Schedule


July 2 Kick Off Meeting and 1st ACT Practice Exam (Baseline)
August 6 2nd ACT Practice Exam
August 31 Start of School
September 3 3rd ACT Practice Exam
September 10 1st ACT Official Exam
October 1 1st SAT Official Exam
October 22 2nd ACT Official Exam (optional)
November 5 2nd SAT Official Exam (optional)


The core program runs for 9 weeks (July 2nd - September 2nd). The typical summer schedule consists of: up to 2 personalized tutoring sessions per week (about 30 minutes each); 5 practice sessions per week (1 hour each); and daily brain training (10 minutes each).


Students take the official ACT exam on Sept. 3rd. They continue studying for the next 4 weeks, averaging about 3 hours per week, and take the first official SAT exam of the fall (October 1st). Students have the option to continue with the program and take the 2nd ACT and SAT exams.


It is expected that students will take a 1 week vacation during the summer during which no studying will be required. InspireDo is able to create custom schedules to accommodate each student’s needs.
