About InspireDo

We focus on motivating and leading students on a path to incredible success with their SAT, ACT, and ultimately college-acceptance goals. Whatever aspirations the students hold, we aim to provide the personalized support necessary to ensure those goals are met. Furthermore, our team strives to provide students with the skills and mindsets that will help them find motivation and success in areas of their lives beyond standarized tests.

The genesis for the company occurred as we went through the college preparation process for Erik and Caroline. We realized that the key component was motivating them to put in the time and effort required for success. Fortunately, Erik and Caroline were able to tap into that motivation, dramatically improve their SAT and ACT scores, and both earned acceptance at one of the top schools in the country, Williams College. Erik is currently a Junior majoring in Computer Science and Caroline is a Freshman.

We leveraged the expertise we garnered from their preparatory process and created InspireDo in January of 2015. Erik created the software application and helped develop the curriculum with Caroline. Caroline provided most of the one-on-one tutoring. Scott leveraged his years of research and experience around self-improvement to create the brain training component. Annika handles the accounting, helps tutor, and now helps Erik with software development. Cameron, an InspireDo student last summer, will be a tutor/coach this coming summer. We are excited to have another wildly successful session this summer!
